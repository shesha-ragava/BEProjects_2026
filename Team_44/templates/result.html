Recommendations

{% if predicted_class == 'Acne and Rosacea Photos' %}

For Acne and Rosacea Management:

- Keep skin clean with gentle, non-comedogenic cleansers

- Avoid known triggers (spicy foods, alcohol, extreme temperatures)

- Use broad-spectrum sunscreen daily (SPF 30+)

- Consider prescription medications like topical antibiotics or retinoids

- Avoid touching or picking at affected areas

- Use lukewarm water for washing (avoid hot or cold extremes)

- Consult a dermatologist for proper treatment plan

{% elif predicted_class == 'Eczema Photos' %}

For Eczema Management:

- Moisturize skin multiple times daily with fragrance-free emollients

- Use gentle, hypoallergenic skincare products

- Avoid known triggers (certain fabrics, soaps, detergents)

- Take lukewarm (not hot) showers or baths

- Use a humidifier in dry environments

- Apply moisturizer immediately after bathing

- Consider prescribed topical corticosteroids when flaring

{% elif predicted_class == 'Melanoma Skin Cancer Nevi and Moles' %}

URGENT: Potential Melanoma Detected

- Schedule an immediate appointment with a dermatologist

- Document any changes in the mole/lesion (size, color, shape)

- Take clear photos for monitoring changes

- Avoid sun exposure to the area

- Use high SPF sunscreen and protective clothing

- Learn the ABCDE rule for melanoma detection

- This requires professional medical evaluation ASAP

{% elif predicted_class == 'Hair Loss Photos Alopecia and other Hair Diseases' %}

For Hair Loss Management:

- Avoid harsh hair treatments and tight hairstyles

- Use gentle, sulfate-free shampoos

- Consider supplements (after consulting healthcare provider)

- Protect scalp from sun exposure

- Maintain a healthy, balanced diet

- Reduce stress through lifestyle changes

- Consult a dermatologist for treatment options (including topical medications or PRP)

{% elif predicted_class == 'Psoriasis pictures Lichen Planus and related diseases' %}

For Psoriasis/Lichen Planus Management:

- Keep skin well-moisturized with thick creams or ointments

- Use medicated shampoos for scalp involvement

- Consider phototherapy treatment

- Avoid skin injury and friction

- Manage stress through relaxation techniques

- Use prescribed topical treatments consistently

- Regular follow-up with dermatologist recommended

{% else %}

- Continue regular skin care routine

- Use sun protection daily

- Monitor any changes in skin condition

- Maintain good general skin hygiene

- Consult a healthcare provider for persistent concerns
