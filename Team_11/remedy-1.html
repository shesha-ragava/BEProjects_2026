Acidity

To neutralize excess stomach acid, relieve burning sensation, bloating, and indigestion.

Options

Option 1 – Jeera Saunf Water

  • Take 1 tsp cumin seeds + 1 tsp fennel seeds.
  • Boil in 1 glass of water for 5 minutes.
  • Strain and drink lukewarm after meals.

Option 2 – Tulsi Honey Drink

  • Crush 4–5 fresh basil (tulsi) leaves.
  • Mix in 1 tsp honey and a little lukewarm water.
  • Drink slowly on an empty stomach.

How to Use

  • Drink once or twice daily when acidity occurs.
  • Jeera-Saunf water is safe after meals.
  • Tulsi-honey is best in the morning.

Precautions

  • Avoid overuse of honey if you’re diabetic.
  • Do not drink very cold water immediately after the remedy.
  • Avoid triggers: oily/spicy food, late-night eating, excessive coffee/tea.
